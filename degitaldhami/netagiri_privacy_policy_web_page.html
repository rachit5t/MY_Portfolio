1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Netagiri ("the app") handles information when you use the game. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly.

Netagiri does not require account registration and does not ask you to submit personal details.

Specifically, we do not collect or store:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Exact location data
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, files, or media from your device
  • User-generated content

However, we use Firebase Analytics to gather anonymized user engagement and gameplay data to help us improve the game experience. This data does not include personal identifying information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.

3. Third-Party Services (Analytics & Advertising)

Although we do not collect personal data ourselves, the app uses Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob for analytics, engagement tracking, and ads.

Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized user engagement and gameplay data to help us understand how players interact with the game and improve the experience. This includes gameplay metrics and user session data, but does not include personal identifying information.

Google AdMob: May collect and process certain information automatically to serve ads and measure performance. This can include device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data. This data is collected and processed by Google under Google's policies, not by us.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You may be able to control ad personalization from your Google settings or device settings (availability depends on device/region).

4. Data Safety & Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our own servers, we do not maintain a database of user personal information.

Any data collected by Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob is handled according to Google's security and privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child's personal information has been collected through a third-party service used by the app, please contact us and we will review the concern.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
